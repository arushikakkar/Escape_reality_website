CORPORATE, TEAM BUILDING AND EVENTS

You don't win with the best talent. You win with the team who are able to play together. Pit your teams with and against each other in Escape Reality's immersive and intensive escape room experience, and let them discover what they're really made of.

BORED OF THE SAME OLD TRIPS OUT WITH WORK? WHY NOT ORGANISE AN ESCAPE ROOM TEAM BUILDING TRIP TO ESCAPE REALITY?

When looking through the list of all the escape games that you could play, there are so many different and unusual scenarios that you could throw your co-workers into. You could put your colleagues behind bars in the infamous prison Alcatraz or ask them to find and capture an evil Vampire named Nosferatu. How about testing their skills of deception and seeing if they can steal an expensive painting in The Heist? If none of those options take your fancy, then they could face their fears in any of our horror themed rooms or become expert detectives in some of our adventure themed rooms.

As an unusual team building activity, teams of between 2-6 players are locked within a themed room for sixty minutes and must work together to find and solve the challenging puzzles. Players will have to think creatively and interact together in a neutral environment, out of the workplace, in their attempts to escape the room. You could think of escape rooms as an excellent form of alternative entertainment. Fun for all ages and abilities, they are a truly unique and exciting activity promoting teamwork and communication.

What can you expect during your escape room team building activity? Large groups will be divided into smaller teams and split between multiple different rooms. This creates a sense of competition between colleagues as they race against each other to be victorious. The experience generates a strong sense of teamwork and brings to light different group dynamics highlighting the strengths and weaknesses of members within the team. A successful escape can create a great sense of achievement and bring players closer together as a stronger team.

You can build your own escape room experience by deciding which rooms you would want to play, and exactly when you want to play them. Each of our locations have numerous different rooms to choose from, all with themes varying from horror and thriller to adventure. Depending upon the location you wish to visit, we can offer bespoke packages to make your time with us even better. These special additions could include an offering of catering options, full exclusivity of the building and bar area, or access to our onsite conference rooms which are available in select locations. Just get in touch with the branch you would like to visit to see what we can offer!

WHAT BENEFITS CAN BE GAINED FROM PLAYING ESCAPE GAMES?

Well, immersing co-workers into a completely neutral and unusual environment is a positive thing and a unique team building activity. There are many benefits to real escape rooms which will become evident as soon as everyone returns to the office or workplace.

IMPROVES COOPERATION

Being immersed inside an unusual and non-traditional environment, away from the office, can vastly improve relationships. Different dynamics of individuals and how personalities can work together will come to the forefront as people find their place within the group. The situation almost exposes potentially hidden characteristics as individuals are in a situation where they cannot hide behind the comfort of their desk. It exposes both the strengths and weaknesses of individuals and how they can take these from each other, combine them and make an effective team.

IMPROVES CO WORKER RELATIONS

Within an escape room, team building activities take on a different form. The setting breaks down social barriers and allow co-workers to mix with different people, such as those who may not be in their department, or by mixing staff members with other managers. This means that bonds and relationships may be strengthened. Any conflicts between employees will be put aside when everyone has the same objective – to escape the room in time! The time deadline will expose who can work better under pressure and calm each other down or reveal those who are a calming influence.

IMPROVES CREATIVE THINKING

When playing an escape room, team building ideas that are monotonous quickly become a thing of the past. Attempts to escape from the room demands the need for creative thinking as the puzzles and mysteries players will have to overcome will differ enormously from those faced in the workplace. The puzzle solving process may not be what is expected, and players will have to combine their logistical thinking in order to solve the puzzles in a quick and efficient manner. Everything players see could be viewed as a potential clue and things may not be as they first seem.
